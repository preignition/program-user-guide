# Customer Portal User Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive user guide for the **Customer Portal**.

Whether you're setting up your profile, managing your billing and subscriptions, organizing your users into teams, or configuring global branding themes, this guide provides all the information you need.

## How this documentation is organized

To help you find exactly what you need when you need it, our documentation is divided into the following sections:

### 1. [Tutorials](./tutorial/index.md) (Learning-oriented)

**Are you a new customer learning to navigate the portal?**  
Our tutorials guide you through the initial setup, including onboarding, configuring your first team, and choosing a subscription plan.

### 2. [How-to Guides](./how-to/index.md) (Goal-oriented)

**Do you need practical directions to complete a specific task?**  
How-to guides help you solve real-world problems. Whether you need to know *"How do I add a new team member?"*, *"How do I change my subscription plan?"*, or *"How do I configure my brand theme?"*, you'll find step-by-step instructions here.

### 3. [Reference](./reference/index.md) (Information-oriented)

**Do you need technical descriptions, facts, or details?**  
The Reference section provides descriptive information about the platform's settings, schemas, and UI elements.

- Start with:
  - [Customer Settings Reference](./reference/customer/index.md)
  - [Team Settings Reference](./reference/team/index.md)

### 4. [Explanation](./explanation/index.md) (Understanding-oriented)

**Do you want to understand the bigger picture or the "why" behind a feature?**  
The Explanation section provides background on concepts like the difference between Customers and Teams, our access control model, and data sovereignty via Storage Locations.

## Getting Started

If you are looking for specific instructions or reference material, simply choose the section above that matches what you are trying to do today!
