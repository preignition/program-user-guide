# Danger Zone

The Danger Zone contains advanced, destructive administrative actions.

> [!WARNING]
> Actions in this section are irreversible. Restricted to Owners and Admins.

## Available Actions

- **Pause Account**: Temporarily suspends customer applications.
- **Cancel Account**: Terminates the subscription and restricts access.
- **Delete Data**: Permanently removes the customer account and all associated data.
