# Training Course - Introduction

This tutorial guides you through creating a 'Training Survey'. Building a survey from scratch is the best way to become familiar with the platform's core features.

> [!NOTE]
> Completing this tutorial and creating the 'Training Survey' will not count against your survey quota.

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

- Build a complete, accessible survey.
- Translate your survey into multiple languages.
- Test and share your survey with participants.
- Analyze responses and export your data.

## Course Structure

The tutorial follows the standard lifecycle of a survey, taking approximately 1 to 2 hours to complete:

1. [**Creating a new survey**](./session-1-creating-a-new-survey.md) (approx. 25 minutes)
2. [**Adding accessibility options**](./session-2-adding-accessibility-options.md) (approx. 15 minutes)
3. [**Translating a survey into different languages**](./session-3-localization.md) (approx. 15 minutes)
4. [**Testing and sharing your survey**](./session-4-distributing.md) (approx. 15 minutes)
5. [**Analyzing responses and exporting data**](./session-5-analyzing-survey-responses.md) (approx. 15 minutes)

## Training Materials

To help you follow along, we provide all the necessary assets:

- Sample text for the 'Introductory' and 'Thank You' pages
- Survey questions
- Easy Read images
- British Sign Language (BSL) videos

Links to these materials are provided in their respective sessions.

> [!TIP]
> We recommend downloading the required training materials to your computer before starting each session.
