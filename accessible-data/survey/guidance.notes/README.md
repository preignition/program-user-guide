# Guidance Notes

## Overview

Guidance Notes provide simple, step-by-step instructions to help you complete common tasks on Accessible Surveys.

Whether you're building your first survey or managing advanced features, these guides will walk you through each process clearly and efficiently.

Each guidance note focuses on a specific task, making it easy to find exactly what you need when you need it.

## What's Covered

The Guidance Notes follow the natural process of creating and managing surveys.  

They cover the following areas:

* **Creating a Survey** - Get started by building a new survey from scratch

* **Editing a Survey** - Make changes and updates to your existing surveys

* **Adding Accessibility Options** - Add features like Easy Read, sign language, read aloud and voice recording

* **Translating a Survey** - Make your survey available in multiple languages

* **Testing a Survey** - Ensure your survey works correctly before sharing it

* **Sharing a Survey** - Publish and distribute your survey to respondents

* **Analyzing Survey Results** - Review survey responses and export your data

* **Managing Your Account and Team Settings** - Configure your organization and team settings and access

## How to Use These Guides

Each guidance note is designed to be self-contained, so you can jump directly to the task you need help with.

Follow the step-by-step instructions, and you'll be able to complete your task quickly and confidently.

If you're new to Accessible Surveys, we strongly recommend starting with our [Training Course](../training-course/README.md) for a comprehensive introduction to the platform.

You can then use these guidance notes if you need more help with a specific task.
