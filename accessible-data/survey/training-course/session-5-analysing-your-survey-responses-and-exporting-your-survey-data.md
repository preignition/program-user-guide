# Session 5- Analyzing your survey responses and exporting your survey data

## Session overview

Previous sessions covered creating your survey, adding accessibility options, translating it into additional languages and testing and sharing your survey.

This final session covers how you can analyze and export your data from the survey's 'Analytics' page.

## Learning outcomes

On completing this session, you should feel confident;

* [x] Using the analytics dashboard to filter your dataset
* [x] Exporting your survey data using different data formats and types of export
* [x] Know how to access on-line help
* [x] Know how to get in touch with us if needed
